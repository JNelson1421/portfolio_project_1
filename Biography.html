Biography

Who am I?

I am 23 years old. I was born and raised in Scottsdale, Arizona. I love listening to music and watching sports. My hobbies include DJing, playing video games and performing magic. I love attending live sporting events and concerts. I go to Colorado on a yearly basis to see some of my favorite artists perform at Red Rocks Ampitheatre. I am currently working on my Bachelors degree in Business Administration. I spent some time in Omaham, Nebraska attending Creighton University then decided to move back home. I then attended Scottsdale Community College for a semester before going to the University of Arizona for a year. After the year the was up I decided to switch to online schooling and get a job. I never knew much about coding until I visited a friend of mine in Colorado who codes. One of the days I was there I shadowed him while he was working from home. I became immediately interested in coding and knew it was something I wanted to explore further.
